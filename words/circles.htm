You try to conform the world
But you realize the world will not conform
So you try to understand the workings of the world
But you realize you can not understand its complexity
So you try to make your peace with the world
But you realize the world can not be trusted
So you try to retire from the world
But you realize your very life depends on it
So you try to live in the world
But you realize that death has been hunting you since birth
So you carry on without worrying too much about it
But you realize that some things are not to your liking
So you try to conform the world ...
