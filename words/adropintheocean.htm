A Drop In The Ocean
A drop in the ocean
a grain on the beach
a star in the sky
just out of reach

a hair on my head
a blade in the grass
a ray from the sun
these things too shall pass

swirling dissolving
uniting again
grief, seperation
prove an illusion

"me" the middle
"you" the outside
distinctions faded
death dissolves pride

now reincarnation
fall, winter to spring
arising in being
from new understanding
