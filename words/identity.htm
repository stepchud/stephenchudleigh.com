Lord, make thyself known to me
For to me, a real sign of thee would surely take more than mere thunder

Lord, resurrect me
For to me, to be found in the ground, I'd surely prefer to stay under

Lord, make use of me
For to me, to be used by thee, would surely be thought a blunder

Lord, identify with me
For to me, identity with thee has surely been cast asunder
