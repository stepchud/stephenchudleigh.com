Staring straight up at the stars,
my body collapses from the weight.
you, me, and the passing cars,
no time to feel, no love or hate.

Bruises tell of untold impacts,
your body shows the scars of time.
we should get past the need for contacts,
seeing clearly thoughts sublime.

Heart to heart we stand together,
bodies at rest but sensing still.
who are we to question weather,
and please ourselves with one more pill.
