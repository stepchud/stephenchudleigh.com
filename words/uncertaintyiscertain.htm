Uncertainty is certain
Of this we need not doubt
"Right"-hidden by a curtain
Left veiled and shaded out

Frustration is my recourse
Solitude is my guide
Justification lacking
It's no wonder i hide

This offers little peace(s)
An island washed by tide
Pieces of the puzzle
Find each other side by side

Who put them there? i wonder
This one does not belong
So fitting is my blunder?
I don't know what went wrong

Confusion is a law, friend
Which language hardly cures
Words and meanings different (insult or compliment?)
Interpretation yours

Some reach out to god for help
Others education
While some may find no comfort
Death answers their question

But we will press on; my hope
A dream of guiding light
Sure to fix the knotted rope
Reason shines banishing night

Take root this precious seedling
Grow, expand, flourish, rise
Newly defined breeding
Uncover all my eyes
