Someday
someday, you'll find a solution
someday, you'll actually try
excuses now are illusions
someday, you'll understand why

someday, you'll love your neighbors
by then it won't seem such a curse
... maybe enjoy your labors
considering what could be worse

someday, you'll understand your religion
instead of just saying "It's True!"
but today don't trouble yourself much
you've more important things to do

someday, that won't sound like derision
you'll use it instead for your gain
but you've always had a decision:
accept love or find in it pain
