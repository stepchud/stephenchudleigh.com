You are in my dreams
but I have not seen you.
I gave you my heart because
I could not stay.

Now we are far apart,
but I can not forget.
You shared yourself with me
and it changed my outlook.

I can not look at another
without seeing your face.
You gave me hope
and now I believe.

Tomorrow it will be the same,
time passes slowly.
One day we will meet again
but I can not say what will happen.

Will we be happy? Will we cry?
Can I hold you like I did
on the beach at Sta. Eulalia?
Or will you shake my hand?
