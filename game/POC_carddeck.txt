Number cards:
=============

Ranks 2-4:  4 of each number in every suit e.g. 4 two of clubs, 4 three of spades, etc.
Ranks 5-7:  3 of each number in every suit
Ranks 8-10: 2 of each number in every suit

Face Cards:
===========

4 Jacks of Spades
3 Jacks of Diamonds
2 Jacks of Clubs
1 Jack of Hearts

2 Queens of Diamonds
1 Queen of Clubs
